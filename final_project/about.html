This is me

Hello, my name is Rasha. I’m sixteen years old and I attend Shaftesbury high school and Manitoba Institute of Technology and Trades, where I am enrolled in the software dev program. I grew up in Toronto, but I moved to Winnipeg a couple years ago. I live with my parents and two sisters. These people are my only family within a thousand-kilometer radius so I’d say we’re pretty close.

Outside of school, you’ll find me taking on new activities and projects. Anything that can stimulate my brain for as long as possible. Recently, I've been into board games, cooking, and non-fiction science books. Some of my more long-term hobbies and interests are graphic design, video editing, and music.
